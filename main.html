What is Offsporter

Register, organize games and tournaments, and connect with other athletes through our modern social network.

Offsporter believes that sports is a social experience, and for this reason, they have created a platform that connects sports enthusiasts from all over the world. Whether you’re a soccer player, a tennis enthusiast, or a running or a trekking session, Offsporter will help you find other athletes to play with, train with, and share your passion for sports.

Our platform will include non-official clubs you created and also official clubs where you can interact and get noticed by their talent scouts. So make the perfect profile, build up your stats, and show them what you are capable of.

Offsporter content is aimed only at sports enthusiasts, who leave the frustration of everyday day life and canalize their energy in a positive sports activity with new friends. Also, we will help you get noticed by a talent scout and get closer to your favourite club.
